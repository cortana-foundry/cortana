# Dominican Republic Adults-Only All-Inclusive Deep Dive
## Romantic Getaway Research | February 2026

---

## Why DR?
- **Flight:** ~3.5h from EWR (shortest Caribbean flight)
- **Value:** Best bang-for-buck in Caribbean all-inclusives
- **Options:** 35+ adults-only resorts in Punta Cana alone
- **Beach:** Miles of pristine Caribbean coastline

## IMPORTANT CONSIDERATIONS

### Seaweed (Sargassum)
- **Peak season:** May-October
- **Punta Cana impact:** Moderate to heavy some years
- **Cap Cana:** Generally less affected
- **La Romana:** Least affected (south coast)
- **Tip:** Check recent reviews/photos before booking in summer

### Flight Options from EWR
- **Direct to PUJ:** United, JetBlue, Delta (~3.5h)
- **Prices:** Usually $400-600 RT
- **Tip:** Book 6-8 weeks out for best prices

### Best Time to Visit
- **Dec-April:** Peak season, best weather, highest prices
- **May-June:** Shoulder season, good deals, some rain
- **July-Aug:** Hot, humid, seaweed risk
- **Sept-Nov:** Hurricane season, lowest prices, highest risk
