#### Verdict

These islands have **very limited accommodation** — small hotels and guesthouses, not luxury resorts.

- Basic accommodations only

#### Verdict
**Not suitable for this trip.** These islands are for eco-adventurers and those seeking off-the-beaten-path experiences. No luxury resorts, limited dining, requires ferry/flights. Perfect for a day trip but not your romantic getaway base.

### Best Time to Book
- **For March 2026:** Book NOW if not already
- March is peak season — high demand, higher prices
- Flights often sell out 9-12 months ahead
- Resorts may have limited availability

### What to Pack
- **Reef-safe sunscreen** — Required by law, enforced
- **Snorkel gear** — Save rental fees
- **Water shoes** — For rocky areas
- **Light resort wear** — Upscale casual for dinners
