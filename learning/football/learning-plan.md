# NFL Learning Plan: Amateur to Pro-Level Knowledge
## February 2026 - September 2026

**Goal:** Transform from NFL amateur to pro-level knowledge by September 2026 season start  
**Duration:** 7 months (Feb-Aug 2026)  
**Context:** Super Bowl LX just concluded (Patriots beat Seahawks) - perfect timing to start your journey!

## Phase 1: Foundation Building (February 2026)

### Week 1-2 (Feb 9-22): Basic Rules & Structure
**Topics:**
- NFL basics: conferences, divisions, playoff structure
- Game rules: downs, scoring, clock management
- Positions and formations (offense, defense, special teams)
- Read Super Bowl LX recap to understand championship-level play

**Media:**
- **Podcasts:** Around the NFL (introductory episodes), The Rich Eisen Show
- **YouTube:** NFL Films channel basics, Brett Kollmann fundamentals videos
- **Books:** "Take Your Eye Off the Ball" by Pat Kirwan

**Practice:**
- Watch Super Bowl LX highlights with newfound knowledge
- Create position group flashcards

### Week 3-4 (Feb 23-Mar 8): Current Landscape
**Topics:**
- 2025 season recap and standings analysis
- Patriots vs Seahawks: How they got to Super Bowl LX
- Key storylines from 2025: MVP race (Maye vs Stafford), surprising teams

**Key Dates:**
- **Feb 23-Mar 2:** NFL Combine (watch QB workouts, learn scouting terms)
- **Mar 3:** Franchise tag deadline

**Media:**
- **Podcasts:** The Athletic NFL Show, PFF NFL Show
- **YouTube:** NFL Network Combine coverage
- **Articles:** The Athletic season reviews, ESPN's 2025 award analysis

**Practice:**
- Follow Combine coverage to learn player evaluation
- Start following 3-4 NFL reporters on Twitter

## Phase 2: Deep Dive Into Teams & Players (March 2026)

### Week 5-6 (Mar 9-22): Free Agency Frenzy
**Topics:**
- How free agency works: salary cap, contracts, dead money
- Major 2026 free agent moves and their impact
- Team building philosophy differences

**Key Dates:**
- **Mar 9-11:** Free agency negotiation period
- **Mar 11:** Free agency begins at 4 PM ET
- **Mar 29-Apr 1:** Annual League Meeting

**Media:**
- **Podcasts:** The GM Shuffle, Over the Cap Podcast
- **YouTube:** Tom Grossi free agency reactions
- **Websites:** OvertheCap.com, Spotrac.com

**Practice:**
- Track 5 major free agent signings and analyze team fits
- Learn basic salary cap concepts

### Week 7-8 (Mar 23-Apr 5): Team Identity Deep Dive
**Topics:**
- Study all 32 team profiles (see team-profiles.md)
- Coaching styles and philosophies
- Historic rivalries and why they matter
- Division dynamics and geographical factors

**Media:**
- **Podcasts:** Team-specific shows for your favorite division
- **YouTube:** Secret Base "Beef History" series, NFL Films documentaries
- **Books:** "America's Game" by Michael MacCambridge

**Practice:**
- Pick one team per division to follow closely (4 total)
- Create rivalry history notes

## Phase 3: Advanced Strategy & Analytics (April 2026)

### Week 9-10 (Apr 6-19): Draft Preparation
**Topics:**
- How the draft works: order, trades, compensatory picks
- Scouting terminology and player evaluation
- Positional value and team needs
- Mock drafts and big boards

**Key Dates:**
- **Apr 15:** Deadline for draft visits
- **Apr 20:** Teams with returning coaches start offseason programs
- **Apr 22:** Various deadlines

**Media:**
- **Podcasts:** The Draft Show, Move the Sticks
- **YouTube:** NFL Draft Network content
- **Websites:** PFF Draft Guide, NFL.com draft coverage

**Practice:**
- Complete 2-3 mock drafts using online simulators
- Learn to evaluate players by position

### Week 11-12 (Apr 20-May 3): Draft Weekend & Analysis
**Topics:**
- Live draft coverage and reaction
- Analyzing team selections and grades
- Understanding reach picks vs. value picks
- Rookie impact projections

**Key Dates:**
- **Apr 23-25:** NFL Draft in Pittsburgh
- **May 1:** Fifth-year option deadline
- **May 1-4 or 8-11:** Rookie minicamps

**Media:**
- **Live Coverage:** NFL Network, ESPN draft coverage
- **Podcasts:** Post-draft analysis episodes
- **YouTube:** Draft reaction videos

**Practice:**
- Grade each team's draft class
- Track rookie minicamp news

- Identify offensive/defensive schemes for your 4 tracked teams
